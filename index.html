About Clasp Trends

Clasp Trend is one of the leading knitted garments and home products export house from India. Located in the Knit city's of Tirupur (India). We have gained a high reputation for our Quality and timely delivery of the products among our clients. We, Clasp Trend are the second generation manufacturer of knit apparel continuing in the tradition of combining native skills and cutting edge technology to produce high end wearable garments. Our production facility which has steadily grown over the last two decades is now vertically integrated with state of the art machinery in all segments of garment manufacturing like spinning, knitting, processing, sewing, printing and embroidery.
